Endodontics

Root canal therapy is directed as a therapeutic procedure for dental bridges. The procedure involves removing the damaged area of the tooth (the pulp) and cleaning and disinfecting it, then filling and sealing it. The common causes affecting the pulp are a cracked tooth, a deep cavity, repeated dental treatment to the tooth or trauma to it. The term "root canal" comes from cleaning of the canals inside the tooth's root.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that corrects teeth and jaws that are positioned improperly. The benefits of orthodontic treatment include a healthier mouth, a more pleasing appearance, and teeth that are more likely to last a lifetime. It is available for teenagers and adults who wish fix teeth and to have perfect look they always wished for.

Orthodontics eliminates:

  • Challenges in maintaining oral hygiene
  • Incidences of dental decay
  • Gum problems
  • Inability to eat and speak efficiently
  • Affecting the person’s appearance

The severity of your problem will determine which orthodontic approach is likely to be the most effective. Improving the function, esthetics, and psychologic well-being is achieved using advanced materials and technologies.
